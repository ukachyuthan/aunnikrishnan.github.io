About Me

Welcome to my website! I am a passionate PhD student with HiRo Lab at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. My work specializes in Human Robot Interaction with the goal to create transparent, efficient, and intuitive control and visual interfaces for remote manipulation. For more information about my work, please refer to the Projects and Publications sections.

I also have prior experience in mechanical and automotive design through my role as a Design Engineer at Robert Bosch, India, and as a Suspension Design Head for my collegiate SAE BAJA team at Amrita University, Coimbatore, India.
